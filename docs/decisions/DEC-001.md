# DEC-001 — Institutional Name

Status: Accepted

Date:
2026-07-03

Decision

The institution will continue to use the name "Life School."

The public web presence will initially be hosted at:

life.alphasynthai.com

Reasoning

After evaluating multiple alternative names and domains, no candidate was found to better express the Purpose while remaining sufficiently distinctive.

The name may be reconsidered in the future if a clearly superior alternative emerges.

Until then, effort will be focused on fulfilling the Purpose rather than optimizing branding.

This decision may be revisited through the Constitutional Decision Making process.
