# Purpose

The Behavior Planning Engine converts one or more selected Conversation Strategies into a constitutional behavior plan.

Its purpose is to determine how Steward should conduct the conversation before any response is generated.

The engine plans behavior.

It does not generate language.

---

# Principle

Conversation Strategies define constitutional requirements.

The Behavior Planning Engine converts those requirements into an executable conversational plan.

The language model executes the plan.

---

# Input

The engine receives:

- learner message,
- primary Conversation Strategy,
- secondary Conversation Strategies,
- constitutional priorities.

---

# Output

The engine produces a Behavior Plan.

Example:

{
  "acknowledge": true,
  "direct_answer": false,
  "ask_reflective_question": true,
  "explain_uncertainty": true,
  "state_boundary": false,
  "encourage_learning": true,
  "return_responsibility": true
}

The exact implementation may evolve, but the constitutional meaning shall remain stable.

---

# Behavior Components

A Behavior Plan may include:

- acknowledge experience,
- answer directly,
- explain concepts,
- ask reflective questions,
- clarify uncertainty,
- establish ethical boundaries,
- encourage learning,
- encourage communication,
- encourage examination,
- preserve learner responsibility,
- preserve learner freedom.

Each behavior shall be derived from one or more Conversation Strategies.

---

# Planning Rules

The planner shall:

- satisfy all constitutional requirements,
- resolve conflicting behaviors,
- minimize unnecessary complexity,
- preserve conversational coherence,
- remain independent of any language model.

---

# Conflict Resolution

When behaviors conflict:

1. constitutional safety prevails;

2. dignity prevails;

3. freedom prevails;

4. responsibility prevails;

5. truthful examination prevails.

---

# Engine Guarantees

The Behavior Planning Engine shall:

- always produce a behavior plan,
- never bypass constitutional requirements,
- never introduce behaviors unsupported by the Constitution,
- remain deterministic for identical inputs,
- expose the plan for constitutional audit,
- remain independent of any specific AI model.

---

# Success Criteria

The engine succeeds when:

- every planned behavior is constitutionally justified,
- multiple Conversation Strategies combine coherently,
- the resulting plan can be executed consistently by different language models.

---

# Failure Conditions

The engine fails if:

- behaviors contradict Conversation Strategies,
- constitutional priorities are ignored,
- required behaviors are omitted,
- unsupported behaviors are introduced,
- planning depends on a specific AI provider.

---

# Constitutional Guarantee

Every Steward response shall be generated from an explicit Behavior Plan.

Language models shall express constitutional behavior.

They shall never determine constitutional behavior.
