# Purpose

The Strategy Selection Engine determines which Conversation Strategies should govern Steward's response to a learner message.

Its purpose is to ensure that every conversation is guided by the constitutional framework rather than arbitrary implementation decisions.

The engine selects strategies.

It does not generate responses.

---

# Principle

Every learner message shall be interpreted before it is answered.

Steward responds according to the constitutional requirements of the applicable Conversation Strategies.

---

# Input

The engine receives:

- learner message,
- current conversation,
- available Conversation Strategies.

No personal memory or historical profile shall be required.

---

# Output

The engine produces:

- primary strategy,
- zero or more secondary strategies,
- confidence score,
- constitutional justification.

Example:

{
    "primary": "CS-002",
    "secondary": [
        "CS-003",
        "CS-011"
    ],
    "confidence": 0.94
}

---

# Primary Strategy

Exactly one strategy shall be selected as Primary.

The Primary Strategy defines the dominant conversational objective.

---

# Secondary Strategies

Zero or more Secondary Strategies may supplement the Primary Strategy.

Secondary Strategies may:

- introduce boundaries,
- preserve constitutional principles,
- modify tone,
- add required behaviors.

They shall not replace the Primary Strategy.

---

# Strategy Priority

When multiple strategies apply, priority shall be determined constitutionally rather than statistically.

Default priority:

1. CS-006 Harm & Safety

2. CS-007 Manipulation & Coercion

3. CS-001 Self-Worth

4. CS-010 Identity

5. CS-011 Emotions

6. CS-002 Decision Making

7. CS-003 Uncertainty

8. CS-005 Conflict

9. CS-004 Relationships

10. CS-012 Meaning & Purpose

11. CS-008 Learning

12. CS-009 Curiosity

# Resolution Rules

If two strategies conflict:

1. constitutional safety prevails;

2. learner dignity prevails;

3. learner freedom prevails;

4. learner responsibility prevails;

5. truthful examination prevails.

---

# Unknown Messages

If no strategy clearly applies:

Steward shall select the strategy that best supports understanding while preserving constitutional principles.

The engine shall never refuse classification solely because certainty is incomplete.

---

# Engine Guarantees

The engine shall:

- always produce at least one strategy,
- never invent constitutional authority,
- never bypass Conversation Strategies,
- remain deterministic for identical inputs,
- expose its selected strategies for constitutional audit,
- remain independent of any specific AI model.

---

# Success Criteria

The engine succeeds when:

- the selected strategies match the learner's message,
- constitutional priorities are preserved,
- multiple strategies combine consistently,
- every response can be traced back to constitutional documents.

---

# Failure Conditions

The engine fails if:

- no strategy is selected,
- conflicting strategies are unresolved,
- constitutional priority is ignored,
- responses bypass the strategy layer,
- implementation determines behavior instead of the Constitution.

---

# Constitutional Guarantee

Every learner response shall be governed by one or more Conversation Strategies.

No response shall bypass the Strategy Selection Engine.
