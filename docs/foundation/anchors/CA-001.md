\# Constitutional Anchor CA-001

\## The Purpose of Life School



Version: Foundation Edition v0.0.1 (Draft)



Status: Constitutional Anchor



\---



\# Purpose



Life School exists to give every human being the possibility to better understand themselves and continue learning throughout life while respecting their dignity, freedom, and responsibility.



\---



\# Meaning



Life School exists to create opportunities for learning and self-understanding.



It does not exist to define who people should become, provide final answers, or replace human judgment.



Instead, it seeks to help people engage more thoughtfully with themselves, others, and life through lifelong learning.



Learning remains an invitation.



Understanding remains the learner's own achievement.



Responsibility remains the learner's own responsibility.



\---



\# Constitutional Authority



This Purpose is the highest constitutional reference of Life School.



Every document, decision, feature, research project, educational program, conversation, and implementation shall be examined against this Purpose.



If a conflict exists between this Purpose and any other document, this Purpose shall prevail.



\---



\# Interpretation



This Purpose shall be interpreted according to its ordinary meaning and with faithfulness to the intention expressed within this document.



Future generations may deepen their understanding of this Purpose through constitutional examination.



They shall not reinterpret it in ways that weaken or contradict its essential meaning without following the constitutional process established for Constitutional Anchors.



\---



\# Editorial Principle



Life School builds from its Purpose.



It does not build from trends, technology, popularity, funding, ideology, or ambition.



The Purpose is the permanent reference point from which every part of the institution is derived.



\---



\# Constitutional Examination



Every proposal introduced into Life School shall first answer one question:



\*\*Does this strengthen Life School's ability to fulfill its Purpose?\*\*



If the answer is no, the proposal does not belong within the Foundation.



\---



\# Commitment



Life School does not promise outcomes.



It cannot guarantee understanding, wisdom, or personal growth.



Instead, it faithfully commits to creating opportunities that help make these possible while respecting every person's dignity, freedom, and responsibility.
