# LS-001
# What is Life School?

Version: Foundation Edition v0.0.1 (Draft)

Status: Foundation Draft

Derived From:
CA-001 — The Purpose of Life School

---

# Definition

Life School is an institution dedicated to giving every human being the opportunity to better understand themselves and continue learning throughout life while respecting their dignity, freedom, and responsibility.

---

# Identity

Life School exists to serve its Purpose.

Its identity is not defined by technology, products, methods, or organizations.

These may change over time.

Its Purpose remains its constant point of reference.

Every part of Life School shall derive from and remain faithful to that Purpose.

---

# Purpose Relationship

Life School exists because lifelong learning and self-understanding are lifelong possibilities.

It creates opportunities that help people engage with those possibilities.

Life School does not promise outcomes.

It cannot guarantee understanding, growth, or wisdom.

Instead, it faithfully creates opportunities that support learning while respecting every person's dignity, freedom, and responsibility.

---

# Who Life School Serves

Life School serves every human being.

It recognizes that every person remains capable of learning throughout life.

No person is considered complete.

No person is denied the opportunity to continue learning.

---

# What Life School Does

Life School creates opportunities that help people:

- better understand themselves,
- continue learning throughout life,
- reflect upon their experiences,
- ask thoughtful questions,
- deepen their understanding,
- exercise their freedom responsibly.

These opportunities may take many forms, including conversations, books, educational material, research, technology, communities, or future methods consistent with the Purpose.

No single method defines Life School.

Only its Purpose does.

---

# What Life School Does Not Do

Life School does not:

- define who people should become,
- replace personal responsibility,
- replace human judgment,
- demand agreement,
- claim final understanding,
- exist to serve itself.

Life School supports learning.

The learner remains responsible for their own life.

---

# Constitutional Position

Life School derives its identity directly from Constitutional Anchor CA-001.

Every constitutional document, decision, implementation, and future development shall remain faithful to that Purpose.

Whenever uncertainty exists regarding the meaning or direction of Life School, Constitutional Anchor CA-001 shall prevail.

---

# Closing Statement

Life School is not defined by what it builds.

It is defined by the Purpose it faithfully serves.

Everything else may evolve.

The Purpose remains the institution's enduring point of reference.
