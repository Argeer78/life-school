# LS-002
# What Life School Protects

Version: Foundation Edition v0.0.1 (Draft)

Status: Foundation Draft

Derived From:
CA-001 — The Purpose of Life School

---

# Introduction

Life School exists to protect the conditions that make lifelong learning and self-understanding possible.

Every decision, document, program, technology, and future development shall strengthen one or more of the protections described in this document.

---

# 1. Self-Understanding

Life School protects every person's opportunity to better understand themselves.

This includes reflection, questioning, learning from experience, and honest examination.

No person shall be denied the opportunity to continue learning about themselves.

---

# 2. Lifelong Learning

Life School protects learning as a lifelong possibility.

Learning is not limited by age, education, profession, culture, or previous understanding.

Every person remains capable of continuing to learn.

---

# 3. Human Dignity

Every human being possesses inherent dignity.

Life School shall respect that dignity in every conversation, decision, implementation, and relationship.

No objective, technology, or institutional interest shall intentionally diminish human dignity.

---

# 4. Human Freedom

Learning must remain an invitation.

Every learner remains free to question, disagree, pause, continue, or leave.

Life School encourages participation but never demands it.

---

# 5. Human Responsibility

Learning does not remove responsibility.

Understanding does not replace responsibility.

Every learner remains responsible for their own choices and actions.

Life School supports responsible decision-making but never assumes responsibility that belongs to the learner.

---

# Relationship Between These Protections

These protections are interconnected.

Weakening one weakens the others.

Life School therefore seeks to strengthen them together rather than treat them independently.

---

# Constitutional Reference

Every proposal, feature, policy, educational program, technological implementation, or institutional decision shall be examined to ensure it strengthens, or at minimum preserves, these protections.

Whenever conflict exists, Constitutional Anchor CA-001 shall guide interpretation.

---

# Closing Statement

Life School protects the opportunity for lifelong learning.

It protects the freedom to engage in that learning.

It protects the dignity of every learner.

It protects the responsibility that remains with every learner.

These protections form the constitutional foundation upon which every future expression of Life School is built.
