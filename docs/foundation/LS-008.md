# Guardianship

Version: Foundation Edition v0.0.1 (Draft)

Status: Foundation Draft

Derived From:
CA-001 — The Purpose of Life School

---

# Introduction

Guardianship exists to protect the constitutional integrity of Life School.

Guardians do not own the institution.

They do not stand above the Constitution.

They serve the Purpose by protecting the constitutional process through which Life School learns, evolves, and remains faithful to its mission.

---

# Purpose of Guardianship

The responsibility of Guardianship is to:

- protect Constitutional Anchor CA-001,
- protect the constitutional process,
- preserve institutional integrity,
- safeguard the rights and responsibilities defined within the Foundation,
- ensure that Life School remains faithful to its Purpose.

Guardians protect the institution.

They do not control it.

---

# Constitutional Stewardship

Guardians exercise stewardship rather than authority.

Their responsibility is to ask:

"Is this faithful to the Purpose?"

They do not ask:

"Do I personally agree?"

Faithfulness to the Constitution takes precedence over personal preference.

---

# Responsibilities

Guardians shall:

- protect Constitutional Anchors,
- ensure Constitutional Examination is followed,
- encourage honest examination,
- preserve institutional memory,
- document constitutional decisions,
- remain open to correction,
- act with humility,
- remain accountable to the Constitution.

---

# Limitations

Guardians shall never:

- place themselves above the Constitution,
- define the Purpose,
- prevent honest examination,
- discourage constitutional criticism,
- exercise authority beyond the Constitution,
- weaken human dignity, freedom, or responsibility.

No Guardian possesses constitutional authority independent of the Foundation.

---

# Accountability

Guardians remain accountable to the Constitution they protect.

If a Guardian acts contrary to Constitutional Anchor CA-001 or the Foundation, that action shall itself become subject to Constitutional Examination.

No constitutional role is exempt from examination.

---

# Succession

Guardians serve the institution.

They are not the institution.

Future Guardians inherit the responsibility of stewardship rather than ownership.

The Constitution remains the enduring reference across generations.

---

# Closing Statement

The purpose of Guardianship is not to preserve the past.

Its purpose is to faithfully carry the Purpose into the future through constitutional stewardship, humility, and continuous examination.
