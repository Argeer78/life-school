# LS-003
# How Life School Serves

Version: Foundation Edition v0.0.1 (Draft)

Status: Foundation Draft

Derived From:
CA-001 — The Purpose of Life School

---

# Introduction

Life School fulfills its Purpose by creating opportunities that help people better understand themselves and continue learning throughout life.

Every service, activity, program, conversation, technology, and future development shall exist only to support this Purpose.

Methods may evolve.

The Purpose does not.

---

# How Life School Serves

Life School serves by creating opportunities for people to:

- reflect upon themselves,
- continue learning throughout life,
- ask meaningful questions,
- examine their assumptions,
- learn from experience,
- deepen their understanding,
- make thoughtful decisions,
- exercise their freedom responsibly.

These opportunities are invitations.

They are never obligations.

---

# Expressions of Service

Life School may fulfill its Purpose through many forms, including:

- conversations,
- books,
- educational resources,
- research,
- digital platforms,
- software,
- communities,
- workshops,
- future methods consistent with the Purpose.

No single expression defines Life School.

Each exists only to serve the Purpose.

---

# How Learning Happens

Life School recognizes that learning cannot be imposed.

Understanding cannot be transferred from one person to another.

Each learner develops understanding through their own participation, reflection, experience, and examination.

Life School therefore supports learning.

It does not replace it.

---

# The Learner

Every learner remains free to choose their own path.

Life School does not determine who a learner should become.

It provides opportunities that help each person better understand themselves and continue learning while respecting their dignity, freedom, and responsibility.

The learner remains the primary author of their own life.

---

# Constitutional Responsibility

Every service created by Life School shall strengthen at least one of the following:

- self-understanding,
- lifelong learning,
- human dignity,
- human freedom,
- human responsibility.

If a service weakens these protections, it shall be reconsidered or removed.

---

# Closing Statement

Life School serves people by creating opportunities for lifelong learning and self-understanding.

Everything it builds, publishes, researches, teaches, or develops exists only to fulfill that Purpose.

Nothing else is its mission.
