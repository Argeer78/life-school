# IL-003
# Revision History

Version: Foundation Edition v0.0.1 (Draft)

Status: Operational Draft

---

# Purpose

Revision History records meaningful institutional revisions and preserves their rationale.

---

# Entry Requirements

Each revision entry should include:

- Revision ID,
- Date,
- Artifact revised,
- Previous position,
- New position,
- Why revised,
- Evidence and reasoning,
- Principle references (FP-001 when applicable),
- Related decisions and dialogues,
- Open uncertainty,
- Next review trigger.

---

# Output

Use templates/RH-ENTRY-TEMPLATE.md.

Maintain entries in registers/revision-history-log.md.
