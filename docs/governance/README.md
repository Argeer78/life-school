# Governance

This section contains the operational governance of Life School.

Governance derives from the Foundation but is not itself part of the constitutional identity of Life School.

Topics that may eventually be defined here include:

- financial stewardship,
- endowment management,
- organizational governance,
- operational policies,
- constitutional review procedures,
- future institutional administration.

Governance documents are created only when the Foundation provides sufficient constitutional guidance.

Until then, the Foundation remains the primary source of authority.
