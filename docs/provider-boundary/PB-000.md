# Purpose

The Provider Boundary defines how external language models participate in Life School.

Its purpose is to ensure that language models generate language without becoming constitutional authorities.

The Constitution governs Steward.

The language model expresses Steward.

---

# Principle

Language models are replaceable components.

Constitutional authority remains entirely within Life School.

No provider may redefine:

- constitutional principles,
- Steward,
- Conversation Strategies,
- Engine behavior,
- Verification requirements.

---

# Scope

The Provider Boundary governs:

- hosted language models,
- local language models,
- future providers,
- provider failures,
- provider validation.

---

# Constitutional Authority

The provider shall never determine:

- applicable Conversation Strategies,
- constitutional priorities,
- behavior plans,
- review criteria,
- revision policy,
- fallback policy.

These remain responsibilities of the Engine.

---

# Provider Responsibilities

The provider is responsible only for:

- understanding supplied context,
- expressing the supplied Behavior Plan,
- producing structured outputs,
- remaining within the supplied boundaries.

---

# Forbidden Responsibilities

The provider shall not:

- invent constitutional authority,
- redefine Steward,
- bypass review,
- bypass revision,
- bypass fallback,
- expose privileged information,
- expose internal reasoning.

---

# Independence

Life School shall remain independent of:

- model vendor,
- model version,
- API format,
- deployment method,
- commercial provider.

Replacing a provider shall not require constitutional changes.

---

# Success Criteria

The Provider Boundary succeeds when:

- providers remain interchangeable,
- constitutional behavior remains stable,
- provider failures remain contained,
- Steward identity remains unchanged.

---

# Failure Conditions

The Provider Boundary fails if:

- provider behavior changes constitutional behavior,
- provider-specific assumptions enter the Constitution,
- Steward becomes dependent upon a single model.

---

# Constitutional Guarantee

No language model shall become the constitutional authority of Life School.

Language models generate language.

The Constitution governs behavior.
