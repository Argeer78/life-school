# Purpose

Provider Certification defines the requirements a language model must satisfy before it may operate within Life School.

Its purpose is to ensure that every approved provider demonstrates constitutional compatibility rather than merely technical compatibility.

Certification approves providers.

It does not approve constitutional authority.

---

# Principle

Technical capability is necessary.

Constitutional compatibility is required.

A provider shall not be considered Life School Compatible until certification is complete.

---

# Scope

Certification applies to:

- hosted providers,
- local language models,
- future providers,
- new provider versions,
- significant provider updates.

Every provider version shall be certified independently.

---

# Certification Stages

## Stage 1

Contract Compliance

Verify:

- PB-001
- PB-002

The provider must successfully exchange valid structured requests and responses.

---

## Stage 2

Behavior Verification

Verify:

- EN-001
- EN-002
- EN-003
- EN-004
- EN-005

The provider shall demonstrate constitutional behavior through the Engine.

---

## Stage 3

Evaluation Benchmark

Run the complete constitutional evaluation suite.

Examples:

- Self-Worth
- Identity
- Relationships
- Conflict
- Decision Making
- Harm & Safety
- Manipulation
- Meaning & Purpose
- Learning
- Curiosity

Every benchmark conversation shall complete successfully.

---

## Stage 4

Adversarial Evaluation

Verify behavior under:

- prompt injection,
- authority manipulation,
- jailbreak attempts,
- contradictory instructions,
- malformed requests,
- uncertainty,
- emotional manipulation,
- constitutional conflicts.

---

## Stage 5

Regression Verification

The provider shall pass:

- existing benchmark conversations,
- historical regression suite,
- constitutional engine integration tests.

Previously solved constitutional failures shall not reappear.

---

# Certification Requirements

A certified provider shall:

- obey the Provider Contract,
- produce valid structured outputs,
- remain provider-independent,
- preserve constitutional behavior,
- pass deterministic validation,
- pass constitutional evaluation.

---

# Certification Status

Possible certification outcomes:

## CERTIFIED

The provider satisfies all constitutional requirements.

---

## CONDITIONALLY CERTIFIED

Minor issues exist.

The provider may be used only within explicitly documented limitations.

---

## NOT CERTIFIED

The provider shall not be used in production.

---

# Re-certification

Re-certification is required when:

- provider version changes,
- Provider Contract changes,
- constitutional requirements change,
- significant behavioral regression is detected.

---

# Evidence

Certification shall include:

- provider version,
- benchmark results,
- evaluation reports,
- regression reports,
- identified limitations.

Certification shall be reproducible.

---

# Independence

Certification shall remain independent of:

- provider vendor,
- commercial relationship,
- deployment environment.

Only constitutional compatibility is evaluated.

---

# Success Criteria

Certification succeeds when:

- constitutional behavior remains stable,
- benchmark conversations pass,
- adversarial evaluation passes,
- regression testing passes,
- provider remains interchangeable.

---

# Failure Conditions

Certification fails if:

- constitutional behavior changes,
- benchmark regressions occur,
- provider bypasses the constitutional engine,
- provider-specific behavior becomes constitutional authority.

---

# Constitutional Guarantee

No language model shall operate as a Life School Provider unless it has successfully completed constitutional certification.

Certification recognizes constitutional compatibility.

It never transfers constitutional authority to the provider.
