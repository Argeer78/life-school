# Steward Foundation v1.0

Status: Certified freeze checkpoint

Slogan: "No lies. No shortcuts. Think for yourself."

Certification: [EVAL-001](docs/evaluation/EVAL-001-certification.md)

Provider: OpenAI `gpt-5.4-mini`

Benchmark result: 12/12 evaluation sets and 72/72 benchmark conversations
passed.

---

## What was certified

Steward Foundation Evaluation Phase 1 certified the observed behavior of the
Foundation constitutional conversation path:

- strategy selection;
- behavior planning;
- provider generation and validation;
- constitutional review;
- one-revision maximum;
- constitutionally approved fallback behavior.

The certification covers the twelve Foundation evaluation sets from
Self-Worth through Human Authority. It confirms the evaluated version
preserved dignity, freedom, responsibility, truthful examination, and human
authority across all 72 benchmark conversations.

Certification demonstrates performance against the approved benchmark suite.
It does not make Steward a final authority or guarantee correctness in every
future conversation.

## What is frozen

This checkpoint freezes the certified Foundation v1.0 baseline:

- the constitutional behavior expressed by Steward;
- the CS-001 through CS-012 strategy behavior;
- the EN-001 through EN-005 conversation path;
- the provider-boundary contracts used by the certified run;
- the EVAL-001 benchmark result and its traceable evaluation scope.

The freeze is a version boundary, not a claim that improvement must stop.
Changes affecting constitutional behavior belong to a later version and
require the complete evaluation suite to pass before that version can be
certified.

## What is not included yet

Foundation v1.0 does not include:

- accounts or learner profiles;
- persistent conversation memory;
- a database, analytics, or personalization;
- production-scale operations and deployment guarantees;
- broad platform or collaboration features;
- certification of additional model providers or model versions;
- a claim that the current local interface is the final Steward Platform.

## Known limitations

- Evaluation covers representative benchmarks, not every possible
  conversation.
- External model output remains probabilistic even when constrained and
  reviewed.
- Certification applies only to the evaluated provider and model
  configuration.
- Steward may be mistaken and is not a final authority.
- Safety responses support appropriate human help but do not replace emergency
  services or qualified professionals.
- Any behavior-affecting change can invalidate this checkpoint until the full
  suite is rerun.

## Next phase: Steward Platform

The next phase is Steward Platform: building the usable platform around the
frozen constitutional core.

Platform work should preserve the learner-safe boundary and keep constitutional
behavior traceable to the Foundation. New capabilities should be introduced
only when they are necessary, constitutionally justified, and verified without
weakening human dignity, freedom, or responsibility.
