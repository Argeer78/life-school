---

# Welcome

Welcome to the Life School repository.

Life School is an institution dedicated to helping people better understand themselves and continue learning throughout life.

This repository is the constitutional and architectural foundation from which every future expression of Life School is derived, including books, educational material, software, research, artificial intelligence, and future initiatives.

The Constitution comes first.

Everything else derives from it.

---

# Purpose

Life School exists to give every human being the opportunity to better understand themselves and continue learning throughout life while respecting their dignity, freedom, and responsibility.

This Purpose is defined by **Constitutional Anchor CA-001**.

# Constitutional Principles

Life School follows several fundamental principles.

- Purpose before implementation.
- Constitution before software.
- Understanding before features.
- Human dignity, freedom, and responsibility are always respected.
- Every significant change is examined before adoption.
- Nothing is considered complete.
- Continuous improvement is part of responsible stewardship.

---

# Contributing

Life School welcomes thoughtful contributions.

Before contributing, please read:

- CONTRIBUTING.md
- COMMUNITY_COVENANT.md

Every contribution should strengthen the Purpose of Life School.

Ideas are examined according to their faithfulness to the Constitution rather than the identity of the contributor.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is one possible expression of Life School.

It is not Life School itself.

Every AI implementation derives from the Constitution and serves the Purpose defined in CA-001.

AI assists learning.

It does not replace human dignity, freedom, responsibility, or judgment.
