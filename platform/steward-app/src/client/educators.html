Frequently asked educator questions

Can it fit short class periods?

Yes. Activities can run in short cycles with prompts, reflection, and brief synthesis.

Can it support assessment?

Yes. Educators can evaluate reasoning quality, clarity, and evidence use with transparent rubrics.

Does it replace teaching?

No. It supports instruction; educators remain responsible for goals, context, and judgment.
