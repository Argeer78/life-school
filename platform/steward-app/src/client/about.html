About Lifeschool

Learn to think. Live with clarity.

Lifeschool is a private learning space where AI helps you examine choices, assumptions, and emotions without replacing your judgment.

Mission

Strengthen self-understanding and lifelong learning through honest examination.

Why Lifeschool exists

Lifeschool exists to help learners examine assumptions clearly and make responsible decisions with dignity, freedom, and accountability.

Who Steward is

Steward is an educational assistant designed to support reflection, not replace human judgment.

What Lifeschool is NOT

Lifeschool is not therapy, emergency support, propaganda, or a final authority over your decisions.

How curriculum works

Each module contains six practical lessons that move from concept to exercise and reflection, then optional guided practice with Steward.

Six current modules

  1. Thinking Clearly
  2. Communicating Clearly
  3. Making Decisions
  4. Understanding Emotions
  5. Relationships
  6. Purpose & Meaning

Future roadmap

Future releases expand multilingual depth, curriculum breadth, and verification tooling while preserving learner safety and constitutional traceability.

Open development philosophy

Development remains transparent, test-driven, and reviewable. We prefer simple, auditable changes over hidden complexity.
