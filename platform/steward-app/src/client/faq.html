FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions about Lifeschool.

Questions

What is Lifeschool?

Lifeschool is an educational platform for clearer thinking, better decisions, and stronger self-understanding.

Who is Steward?

Steward is Lifeschool's educational guide that helps learners examine ideas and assumptions without replacing human judgment.

Is Steward AI?

Yes. Steward uses AI to support reflection and learning conversations under educational boundaries.

Is this therapy?

No. Lifeschool is educational, not therapy or medical care.

Is this coaching?

No. Lifeschool provides educational guidance, not personal coaching services.

Is my data stored?

Lifeschool stores only limited local preferences and does not provide persistent learner account history.

Can schools use Lifeschool?

Yes. Schools can use Lifeschool as a thinking and reflection learning companion.

Can universities partner?

Yes. Universities and educational institutions can discuss partnerships through the contact channel.

Is Lifeschool free?

Core learning access is currently available without paid accounts during this stage.

Why no accounts?

The current design prioritizes simplicity, privacy, and learner-first access without account friction.

Will more modules come?

Yes. More modules are planned and expanded over time through roadmap milestones.

Can I contribute?

Yes. You can contribute feedback, issues, and ideas through official contact channels.

Can I support Lifeschool?

Yes. Visit the support page for collaboration and support pathways.

Can organizations partner?

Yes. Organizations can partner with Lifeschool while respecting educational independence.
