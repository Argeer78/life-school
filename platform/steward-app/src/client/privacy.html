Privacy Policy

Your conversation is your space

This page explains what Lifeschool stores locally, what it sends to external processors, and what it does not retain.

Session storage in browser memory

Learner conversation text is kept in browser memory for the current tab session so you can continue the active exchange.

Local language preference

Lifeschool stores only your language choice locally to keep interface labels in your preferred locale.

No learner history stored

Lifeschool does not create learner accounts and does not persist learner chat history in the client application.

OpenAI processing

Learner prompts are processed by an external model provider for response generation. OpenAI.

Contact form processing

Contact requests are processed to respond to your inquiry and maintain service reliability, including abuse and rate-limit controls.

Cookie and storage choices

Lifeschool does not use analytics cookies or advertising cookies. The cookie banner records only your Accept or Decline choice.
