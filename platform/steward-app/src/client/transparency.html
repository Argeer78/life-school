Transparency

Governance and Transparency

A professional governance overview of how Lifeschool is organized, funded, and reviewed.

Mission

Lifeschool serves self-understanding, lifelong learning, dignity, freedom, and responsibility through educational practice.

Governance

Governance follows constitutional hierarchy and document traceability so major decisions remain reviewable and justified.

Funding

Funding supports educational infrastructure and development, without authority over curriculum principles or learner-facing direction.

Supporters

Supporters are acknowledged transparently according to public criteria and cannot purchase influence over educational standards.

Privacy

Privacy design minimizes stored learner data and clearly states what is processed, retained, and excluded from tracking.

AI Usage

AI is used as an educational support tool with strict role boundaries, preserving human judgment and responsibility.

Educational independence

Institutional partnerships, funding, and tooling do not determine educational truth claims, learner decisions, or constitutional commitments.

Open development

Development is test-driven, documented, and reviewable, favoring explicit reasoning over hidden complexity.

Transparency reports

Lifeschool publishes report-style updates on roadmap progress, boundaries, and key production changes.

Future annual reports

Future annual reporting will summarize educational progress, governance changes, and platform accountability indicators.
